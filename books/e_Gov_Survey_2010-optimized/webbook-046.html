To collect information
E-government can provide information tools for
regulators to develop and then implement regu-
lations related to the shadow banking system.
Reform of the shadow banking system should
aim to put the system back on the radar screen of
regulators. Most requirements to the deposit banks
would be transplanted to the shadow banking sys-
tem, such as the requirement of adequate capital
reserves, liquidity, and so on.
Regulators need to uncover more informa-
tion compared with what has to be supplied
under the existing regulations. The activities of
banking are highly connected, and their trans-
actions, as previously emphasized, do not hap-
pen in traditional ways, i.e. in regulated venues
and via transparent counterparties. Therefore,
new categories of information should be ex-
