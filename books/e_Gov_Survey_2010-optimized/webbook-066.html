Recent actions
E-health, a process of administering health care
through ICT tools, is rapidly growing in impor-
tance in all parts of the world. Telemedicine, for
example, is a process of accessing health care from a
distance through ICT tools. Telemedicine has been
successfully implemented in several developing
countries, including Bhutan, Ghana, Indonesia,
Niger, Nigeria, Pakistan, Peru and South Africa.
Electronic health records are another example
of ICT use in this sector. e stimulus package in
the United States contains $19 billion for health
care technology spending and the adoption of elec-
tronic health records.
